AMSKU Residency Program

Engagement Workflow & Foundations Pathway Strategy

Foundations Strategy

  • HT Weekend Priority: First 2 weekends scheduled up-front.
  • Orientation call is the primary trigger.
  • Schedule HT in conjunction with VT.

Operational Pillars

  • Weekly Tuesday Collaborative Sessions
  • Brianne: Primary Contact for scheduling & Tuesday sessions.
  • Automated Progress Reports (VT/HT Status).
